My project, "Speak and Read," encompasses two core functionalities:

# Speech-to-Text: This feature listens to user speech and converts it into text. It enables real-time transcription of spoken words, which is particularly useful for users who need assistance with communication or documentation.

The goal of "Speak and Read" is to make communication more accessible and intuitive for everyone.
